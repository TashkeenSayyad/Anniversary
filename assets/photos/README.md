# Photographs

Drop your memories here.

**Recommended specs**
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Dimensions: 1200–1600 px on the long edge (balances quality and load time)
- Orientation: a mix of portrait and landscape looks best in the gallery

**Suggested filenames** (to match the default gallery layout):
- `nikah.jpg` — your wedding day
- `favourite.jpg` — a favourite moment together
- `us.jpg` — a portrait of the two of you

Then open `index.html` and swap each `<div class="frame-placeholder">…</div>` in the gallery section for:

```html
<img src="assets/photos/nikah.jpg" alt="Our nikah." />
```
