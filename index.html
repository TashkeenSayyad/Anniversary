Bismillāhir-Raḥmānir-Raḥīm

365 flowers for 365 blessed days of being married to you.

Alhamdulillah, Allah blessed my life with your love and filled my heart with peace through you. In you, I found tenderness, comfort, and a home for my heart. Every day with you has been a mercy, and every memory with you has become something I treasure deeply.

On this first anniversary, I want you to know that my love for you is not only in this day, but in every prayer I make for us, in every hope I carry for our future, and in every quiet moment where I thank Allah for writing you for me.

Three moments to mark

  1. I

    The day we became husband and wife — that day changed my life forever, and from that moment on, my heart carried your name with even more love.

  2. II

    The little moments only we know — the quiet talks, the smiles, the comfort, the softness of your presence.

  3. III

    The future I dream of with you — I pray for a lifetime of love, mercy, barakah, and nearness between our hearts.

A dua

May Allah keep our hearts joined in love, mercy, and barakah. May He protect our marriage, deepen our affection, and bless us with many beautiful years together, in this life and the next. Ameen.

Thank you for every smile, every prayer, every memory, and every moment. I love you more with each passing day.

Forever yours.
